About Rivet & Reprieve

We started with a single roll of Japanese selvedge denim and a stubborn belief that jeans should last decades, not seasons. Every pair is cut, sewn, and finished in our workshop in Los Angeles.

Rivet stands for strength – the copper rivets that hold stress points together. Reprieve is the comfort you earn after breaking them in.

Our ethos

  • 100% cotton, no stretch, no shortcuts
  • Ethical production, fair wages
  • Repair any pair, for life

We’re denim nerds who believe in slow fashion. Thanks for stopping by.
