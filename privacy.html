Privacy policy

Your data is yours. We collect only what’s needed to get your denim to you — and we treat it with the same care we put into every pair of jeans.

Our commitment

Rivet & Reprieve respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or make a purchase. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

GDPR compliant encrypted

What we collect

  • Name & email – for orders and shipping updates
  • Shipping address – to deliver your jeans
  • Payment info – processed securely via Stripe (we never store card details)
  • Browsing behaviour – anonymous analytics to improve our store

We collect minimal data — only what’s essential.

How we use it

  • Fulfill orders – process and ship your purchase
  • Send updates – order confirmation, tracking, and occasional style news (you can opt out)
  • Improve experience – analyse site usage to make our store better
  • Customer support – respond to your questions and requests

We never share your data with advertisers.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies for cart and checkout. No tracking cookies. You can disable them anytime.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data anytime. Just email us at privacy@rivetreprieve.com.

Data retention

We keep order data for 5 years (for tax and warranty purposes). You can request earlier deletion.

Questions? We're here to help. Contact us at privacy@rivetreprieve.com or via our contact page.
