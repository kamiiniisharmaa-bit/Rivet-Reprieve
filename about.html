About Rivet & Reprieve

We believe in denim that outlasts trends — made with integrity, worn with character.

Our story

Founded in 2018, Rivet & Reprieve started with a single roll of Japanese selvedge and a stubborn belief that jeans should last decades, not seasons. Every pair is cut, sewn, and finished in our Los Angeles workshop.

Made to last

We use 100% cotton, copper rivets, and triple-stitched seams. No stretch, no shortcuts — just honest denim that ages with you. Our repair program keeps your favourite pair alive for life.

Our ethos

  • 100% cotton · no stretch, no synthetics
  • Ethical production · fair wages, safe conditions
  • Lifetime repair · we fix any pair, any age
  • Slow fashion · small batches, zero waste

We’re denim nerds who believe that the best jeans are the ones you never want to take off.

Japanese selvedge

Sourced from Kaihara mills, known for deep indigo and tight weave.

Signature fits

Slim, straight, and loose — each cut engineered for movement and durability.

Repair program

Send us your worn pair — we’ll darn, patch, and reinforce for free.
